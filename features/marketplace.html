Integrated Local Marketplace

Feature Overview

The Integrated Local Marketplace is a digital platform within Pravas that connects tourists directly with local service providers, artisans, and businesses in Jharkhand. This feature aims to promote authentic local experiences, support the local economy, and ensure fair compensation for community members while providing tourists with unique products and services.

Key Components

1. Service Provider Listings

Types of Listings:

  • Accommodations: Homestays, eco-lodges, and traditional houses
  • Local Guides: Specialized guides for different experiences (wildlife, trekking, cultural)
  • Transportation: Local drivers, bicycle rentals, traditional transport options

Listing Features:

  • Detailed profiles with photos and descriptions
  • Blockchain-verified credentials and certifications
  • Availability calendars and booking options
  • Transparent pricing and payment terms
  • Reviews and ratings from previous tourists

2. Handicraft Marketplace

Product Categories:

  • Traditional tribal art and paintings
  • Handwoven textiles and clothing
  • Wooden crafts and bamboo products
  • Tribal jewelry and ornaments
  • Pottery and ceramic items
  • Local food products and spices

Product Features:

  • High-quality images and detailed descriptions
  • Information about the artisan and crafting process
  • Cultural significance and history of the craft
  • Shipping options or in-person collection details

3. Local Events & Festivals

  • Calendar of local cultural events and festivals
  • Ticket booking for performances and ceremonies
  • Special packages for festival experiences
  • Information about traditions and customs
  • Photography and participation guidelines

How It Works

For Service Providers & Artisans

  1. Registration & Verification:
    • Local providers register with basic information
    • Undergo verification process (identity, skills, certifications)
    • Receive blockchain-based verification certificate
    • Create detailed profile and listing
  2. Listing Management:
    • Upload photos and descriptions of services/products
    • Set pricing and availability
    • Specify booking terms and conditions
    • Update inventory and availability in real-time
  3. Order & Booking Management:
    • Receive booking notifications
    • Confirm or decline booking requests
    • Communicate with tourists through secure messaging
    • Receive payments through blockchain escrow system
  4. Post-Service:
    • Confirm service completion
    • Receive payment from smart contract
    • Request reviews from tourists
    • Access analytics about their business performance

For Tourists

  1. Discovery:
    • Browse categories of local services and products
    • Search with filters (location, price, rating, type)
    • View recommended providers based on preferences
    • Explore featured artisans and unique experiences
  2. Selection & Booking:
    • View detailed information and verified reviews
    • Check availability for desired dates
    • Make booking request or purchase products
    • Secure payment through blockchain system
  3. Experience:
    • Receive booking confirmation and details
    • Access directions and contact information
    • Communicate with service providers if needed
    • Use the app for translation and cultural information
  4. Post-Experience:
    • Confirm service completion
    • Release payment from smart contract
    • Leave reviews and ratings

Technologies Used

  • E-commerce Platform: Custom marketplace functionality with secure transactions
  • Blockchain: Smart contracts for escrow payments and NFT certificates
  • Recommendation Engine: AI-based system to suggest relevant products and services
  • Messaging System: Secure in-app communication between tourists and providers
  • Calendar Integration: Real-time availability management
  • Payment Gateway: Multiple payment options with blockchain security

Benefits

For Local Communities

  • Direct Economic Benefits: Income flows directly to local providers without intermediaries
  • Cultural Preservation: Incentivizes continuation of traditional crafts and practices
  • Digital Inclusion: Brings local businesses into the digital economy
  • Fair Pricing: Providers can set their own prices based on fair value
  • Skill Development: Training in digital marketing and business management

For Tourists

  • Authentic Experiences: Direct access to genuine local experiences and products
  • Quality Assurance: Verified providers and blockchain-certified products
  • Cultural Connection: Deeper understanding of local traditions and crafts
  • Convenience: One-stop platform for all local services and products
  • Responsible Tourism: Ability to support local communities directly
